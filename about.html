ABOUT US

The Semiotones are a charity band based at Victoria College in the University of Toronto. Under the direction of Professor Marcel Danesi, The Semiotones have been sending all proceeds from their concerts and music sales to the SickKids Foundation since the band’s founding in 2008. Comprised of alumni, a tenured professor, and current students, the band plays music that spans a wide variety of genres, including jazz, Latin, blues, and 1950s rock ‘n’ roll, ballads, and boogie-woogie. With an array of instrumentalists and vocalists, The Semiotones boast a unique and powerful sound that both excites and soothes the soul. Performing in various venues and recording throughout the year, the band aims to entertain while supporting a fantastic cause.

The band has achieved an international reputation with their original music, playing throughout Toronto and abroad, most recently at the famous LongLake Festival in Lugano, Switzerland. The Semiotones have also been featured on CBC radio, CIUT radio, and Omni 1.
